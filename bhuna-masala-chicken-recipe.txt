Ingredients:
 -1/2 kg chicken wings
 -2 onions finely julienned
 -3 tomatoes sliced
 -1 capsicum finely julienned
 -1 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
 -1 tsp turmeric powder
 -1 tsp red chili powder
 -1 1/2 tsp coriander powder
 -1 1/2 tsp cumin powder
 -3 dry red chili
 -1/2 tsp cumin seeds
 -1/2 tsp coriander seeds
 -1/2 tsp sugar
 -1/2 cup water
 -1/2 lime juice
 -Salt to taste
 -2 tsp refined oil
 -1 Tbsp olive oil

 Directions:
  -Marinate the chicken wings.
  -Drizzle the chicken wings with olive oil. Then add ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, chili powder,
   coriander powder, and cumin powder, salt to taste and massage the chicken wings gently.
  -Heat up the pan and drizzle with one tsp oil and sear the wings in it, add salt to taste.
 For bhuna masala:
  -Heat up the pan and drizzle it with 1 tsp of oil and add dry red chili, cumin seeds and coriander seeds.
  -Saute it lightly. Then mix chicken wings with the bhuna masala.
  -Add onions and saute it till golden brown.
  -Add tomatoes and cook for 2-3 minutes. Then add sugar, water, lime and capsicum and let 
   it cook away for 2-3 minutes more.
  -Sprinkle chopped coriander leaves to garnish.
